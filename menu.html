Menu

At Délice, we believe in creating an unparalleled dining experience where every detail is crafted to perfection. Our menu is designed not just to satisfy, but to transcend your expectations, offering a journey through the finest flavors and textures.

Indulgence Package

For those who seek the ultimate luxury, our Indulgence Package is the perfect choice. Enjoy a selection of three to four of your favorite desserts from our curated menu, each paired with a carefully chosen alcoholic beverage that complements and enhances the flavors. Whether it's a fine wine, a handcrafted cocktail, or a rare spirit, each drink is selected to create a harmonious pairing with your desserts.

Price: 1499 SEK

Pure Delight Package

For a non-alcoholic yet equally luxurious experience, our Pure Delight Package offers the same exquisite selection of three to four desserts, paired with refreshing non-alcoholic beverages. From artisanal mocktails to rare teas and freshly pressed juices, each drink is chosen to elevate your dessert experience to new heights.

Price: 998 SEK
